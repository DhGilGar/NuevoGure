Gurecard

Prepaid Cards with online management

Characteristics
  • Payments of mechanical repairs, fines, accommodation, tolls, any necessary payment.
  • Products or services online purchases.
  • Cash withdrawals at ATMs.
  • No intermediaries.
Loading System Functionality
  • Request new Gurecard.
  • Instant card loading and unloading.
  • Ferries, tunnels and bridges booking.
  • Guretruck invoices payment.
With the Gurecard- Prepaid cards you will have the convenience of a card but without the need to associate a bank account or change banks. They are ideal to distribute among your drivers.
  • Request your Gurecards from www. truckcontroller.com in a quick and easy way receive them at your desired address.
  • Reload your Virtual Account and transfer money to your Gurecards.
  • Distribute the cards to your drivers.
  • Consult the balance on your cards and card movements.
  • Complete integration with TMS.
  • Direct allocation of expenses in an automatic way for accounting.
  • Consult you PIN and activate alerts.
  • In case of loss or theft, simply unload the card balance and block it from your Truck Controller Virtual Account.
  • Contactless technology allows payment by simply holding the card over the sales terminal to make payment wirelessly, avoiding any type of contact.Manage your money securely and with piece of mind.
