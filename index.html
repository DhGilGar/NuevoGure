About

Always one step ahead towars excellence.

Founded in 1996

Guretruck S.L. is a multi-disciplinary firm specializing in providing services to the European road transport industry. We provide a full range of solutions including legal services, assistances, fleet management, positioning, VAT services, pre-paid cards and tachograph management services. We provide national and international expertise to meet our client’s needs in any European jurisdiction.

The interaction between our clients and the professionals at Guretruck is key to our development and growth and is what makes us competitive in an increasingly complex market. We focus on client needs and really try to understand how their businesses work; this allows us to provide more tailored solutions.

Truck Controller Ecosystem

Due to popular demand, Guretruck designed and built an online platform (www.truckcontroller.com) which houses together all of its services under one roof. Via Truckcontroller you can pay road transport fines, request legal services, control your fleet, track the progress of your loads or refund VAT.
