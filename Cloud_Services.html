Cloud Computing Services

Cloud-based collaborative tools

Document Box
Allows you to store and share your company’s documents remotely and securely.
  1. Share files with employees or external users.
  2. View your files at any time.
  3. Better document management.
  4. Up to 500 GB of storage.
  5. Wide variety of systems and formats.
  6. Protection of all documents.
Shared Calendar
The easiest way for teams to plan, manage and visualise their work through a shared team calendar.
  1. Share agendas with your team.
  2. Respond to invitations.
  3. Show your andyour teammates work week.
  4. Identify absences.
  5. Visualise and share events and deadlines of your projects
  6. Schedule meetings with potential clients.
This solution provides transport companies with a comprehensive, easy-to-use platform for managing documents, coordinating teams and meeting deadlines, all in one place.
  • Centralisation and Remote Access: All documents and calendars are integrated in one place, allowing you to access and manage information from any device with an internet connection.
  • Real-Time Collaboration: Facilitates collaboration between team members, who can share files, update the calendar and coordinate their tasks regardless of their location.
  • Secure, Scalable Storage: Offers up to 500 GB of document storage, with multiple layers of security to protect sensitive information.
  • Compatibility with Different Systems and Formats: Allows files to be stored and shared in various formats, ensuring flexibility for different types of documents.
  • Efficient Project Management: The shared calendar makes it easy to schedule meetings, assign tasks and track important dates, optimising the management of team time and resources.
  • Information Protection: Includes security tools to protect documents from unauthorised access, ensuring that company information is always secure.
  • Full Integration within Truck Controller: All these tools are fully integrated within TruckController, allowing data to flow between different modules without the need for manual synchronisation, optimising management processes and avoiding duplication errors or data loss.
